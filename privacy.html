Our Privacy Commitment

Transparency and trust in how we handle your personal information

Privacy Policy

At Serene Brews Cafe, your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our cafe, use our website, or participate in our rewards program.

Information We Collect

To provide you with the best possible experience, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number when you sign up for our rewards program or newsletter.
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history and preferences when using our rewards program.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and operating system when you visit our website.
  • Location Data: With your permission, to help you find the nearest Serene Brews location.
  • Feedback: Your comments and suggestions from surveys or reviews.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information responsibly to enhance your experience with us:

  • Process your orders and manage your rewards account
  • Personalize your experience and recommend products you might enjoy
  • Improve our services, menu offerings, and customer experience
  • Communicate with you about promotions, events, and updates (with your consent)
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide exceptional customer service
  • Ensure the security of our services and prevent fraud

Information Sharing

We value your trust and do not sell your personal information. We may share data in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help with payment processing, email delivery, and analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: When necessary to comply with laws or respond to legal requests.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
  • With Your Consent: For specific purposes you explicitly agree to.

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services we request.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login information
  • Understand how visitors use our site to improve it
  • Deliver personalized content and offers
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect some website features.

Your Privacy Rights

You have control over your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your information
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Policy Updates

We may occasionally update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We'll notify you of significant changes through our website or email.
